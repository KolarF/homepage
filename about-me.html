Hi, I'm Fabio.

I'm a trained historian with a Master of Arts degree from University of Hamburg. Together with my family, I live on the northern fringes of Hamburg.

Currently, I'm striving for a career in Web Development, while working part-time in Customer-Relationship-Management for the SPIEGEL Group. I'm learning to code with Codecadmey and support my learning process with the study of corresponding literature.

Before, I have worked in different professional fields. I taught at University of Hamburg and also at Flottkamp, a primary school in Schleswig-Holstein. At IU International University of Applied Sciences, I had a job in Administration, for the REWE Group, I worked in Logistics.

I love spending time with my family and friends. Apart from that, I enjoy playing chess, online or over the board. Cooking is a passion of mine as well, and I have a particular weakness for seafood. Literature is important to me. One of my favorite books is Jaume Cabré's "Confessions". I laugh a lot and love the quirky humor of series like "Bored to death" and "Broad City".

Mexican history is another interest of mine. Having a Spanish background, I wanted to improve my Spanish speaking skills during university. Therefore, I took the opportunity to spend a semester abroad at the Colegio de México and was fascinated with the culture and history of Mexico. For my not yet finished PhD project on Intellectual History in Mexico, I returned twice - and I would love to take another trip in the future.
